iTunes has forever changed the way you live music, movies and podcasts. Now it’s time for a new revolution: three new apps - Music, Apple TV, and Podcast - are coming to your Mac, designed to bring you the best in entertainment. Don’t worry: everything you have in your iTunes library is always available in individual apps and, as always, iCloud syncs everything on all your devices. Or, if you prefer, you can back up your content, restore it, and sync it by connecting your device directly to your Mac.
